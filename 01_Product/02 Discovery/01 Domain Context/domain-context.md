## Current process (before the product)

Design teams often start from scratch per project: no shared template, ad‑hoc specs, and context lost between phases. The “product” here is the **core template** that future design projects will clone.

### Identified friction points

- [HYPOTHESIS] Teams duplicate setup (personas, brief, tokens) every time.
- [HYPOTHESIS] Specs and screens get out of sync because there’s no single map.
- [HYPOTHESIS] Review is subjective without a clear spec baseline.

## Domain constraints

| Constraint                                                  | Type      | Impact on product                   |
| ----------------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Must stay tool-agnostic (Cursor/Claude today, others later) | Technical | No hard lock-in to one AI or editor |
| Template must work for MVP and Epic intents                 | Business  | Modes LIGHT vs STANDARD per phase   |
| One active module at a time                                 | Business  | Simpler context, clear ownership    |
