## Identified risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |

| Template is too light and teams add their own structure anyway | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Validate with one real project; add only what they ask for. |

## Top 3 hypotheses to validate first

1. **P1 — Setup waste**  
   If true, the template directly addresses it. Validate by using it for one real project and comparing “time to first spec” vs. current ad‑hoc approach.

2. **S1 — Minimal flow is enough**  
   The riskiest: the whole MVP assumes one E2E flow is sufficient. Validate by running /discovery → /ux → /spec → /build → /review once and checking if the outcome is usable.

3. **U1 — Primary user**  
   Who actually clones and runs the template affects what to simplify or document. Validate by naming the first user and one project before investing in more content.
